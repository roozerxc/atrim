# IMPORTANT NOTICE TO RESIDENTS OF THE STATES OF CALIFORNIA AND COLORADO IN THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
As a consequence of recent legislative activity in [California][cal] and [Colorado][col]:

- California residents may no longer use atrim after Jan 1st, 2027.

- Colorado residents may no longer use atrim after Jan 1st, 2028.

The atrim project is probably a software application under these laws. However, it does not, cannot and will not implement age verification.

[cal]: https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=202520260AB1043
[col]: https://leg.colorado.gov/bill_files/111670/download
